My Essential Question:
How can we improve the system that our community uses for service opportunities?

Why do you want to undertake the challenge reflected in this EQ? What about this EQ appeals to you?

While mowing my lawn the other day, I was trying to figure out what I wanted to do for my senior project. I knew that I wanted to do something regarding programming, but my initial thought was a website to teach programming. Then sporadically a video I watched popped into my head. A school who held an event to mow the lawns of their town's elder residents. I started to move my idea from teaching people to program, to helping the community and elderly. Once I began to think and ponder on the idea, I changed it into helping people with community service opportunities. I really liked the idea of helping people, and having an easier means of doing so would be the greatest way to impact those in need.

How does this question demonstrate the need for substantial research? What new knowledge do you need to gain?

The essential question is very broad so we can create a heap of research questions. This topic of changing the community service system hasn't really been reasearched, so we have a lot of new knowledge to gain. Once we learn this knowledge we can construct a solution to greatly improve our system.

Explain how/why your EQ cannot be answered through secondary research alone. How does it require you to apply new knowledge? What original thinking is required?

My EQ cannot be answered thought secondary research alone because it's a very unique question that hasn't been vastly researched. We haven't changed the community service system itself in a while, and doing so while applying new knowledge could vastly improve the opportunities and lives of those in need. We have to think originally about a new system that would work for every party in the service communities.

Here is a short list of some research questions:
- Since there is no graduation requirement, how will we inspire people to do community service?
- Could we have a community service challenge amongst grades?
- How can we reach more people in the community?
- What features need to be added in so that schools would use this new system?

This idea would challenge my programming knowledge and make me research into mobile applications.
