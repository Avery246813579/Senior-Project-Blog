What type of service did I choose?

I choose Indirect Service. The reason I choose Indirect Service is because I wanted my project to impact people. I personally think it's the most efficient way to impact the most people.

What's my essential question?

In what ways does the community display community service opportunites, and in what ways can it be improve?

What content will my project be on?

My project will be on helping communities manage service opportunities. I want to make an easier and more efficient way to find, host, and track communitiy service, and my project will be the answer.

What community and community needs does my project address?

The primary group my project addresses is the community service community. More in particular I will be addressing the need for easier and more available ways to host, find, and track community service opportunities.

What is my service name and slogan?

Commservus: Find, host, and track your community's service needs.

I will be learning new programming skills and also improving my past knowledge. In addition to these obvious skills, I will also be learn more about community service and how it impacts the community.
